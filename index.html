About me

Hello, my name is Julia, I am 20 years old, at the moment I live in Lviv. I love to draw, create designs, shoot videos and take photos. It all inspires and makes me happy. Every day I try to learn something new and get inspired by interesting and talented people. I study at LNAM at the Department of Graphic Design. I also love to travel, travel brings me a lot of energy to create something new and creative.

I love drawing but what I love the most is when my characters make people smile. For me, the main goal of drawing is to have fun with it and never take it too seriously! I have mastered techniques of digital art, vector, manual techniques and apply them actively in my work.

Another hobby of mine is stop motion animation. Stop motion is very popular and it looks spectacular in my opinion.


Works

illustration for #bottlechalange

I recently took part in the # Bottle Challenge, this challenge lasted a month and it turned out to be very useful for me, it develops imagination and a useful habit of drawing every day.

vpodobayka coffe - branding

The main task was to create a logo and identity for the coffee shop. Vdobayka is a new ambitious establishment that combines two stories: coffee culture and sweet without sugar. The visual style was created and selected to attract attention with its uniqueness and friendliness both in the combination of colors and in the simplicity of figures and elements.

photo

Photography has recently become my hobby, I like to come up with ideas for shooting, prepare props and retouch.I loved subject photography, photojournalism, I also like genre photography.

font "mountain"

Mountain is the first font. which I developed myself. Font design is a therapeutic experiment for me, I make mistakes, but nothing is perfect. But I'm learning...
